🪞 Axis of Symmetry

Fold & See! — Try folding along different lines to see which halves match perfectly

Axis of Symmetry is a line that divides a shape into 2 equal halves. If you fold along this line and both sides overlap perfectly, that line is an axis of symmetry for the shape! 🎉
Some shapes have more than one axis of symmetry — for example, an isosceles triangle has 1, a rectangle has 2, an equilateral triangle has 3, and a square has as many as 4! Try selecting different fold lines and see for yourself.
